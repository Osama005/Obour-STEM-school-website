Campus life

Obour STEM school is built on a fairly broad piece of land including the academic amenities, students’ habitations, and playgrounds to serve the students. The school consists of three main areas:


School Building
School Residence
School Labs

School Building

The school building has one of the unique construction designs among Egyptian schools. Including 18 classes, distributed fairly among boys and girls. Inside these classrooms, students are separated into 5 separate groups to enhance teamwork and facilitate sharing knowledge without any external help. Groups are meant to enhance their sense of competition which encourages students to improve their skills.
Although the school focuses on the refinement of the students' academic skills, Sports play a leading role in keeping the students' shape motivate and release the excess energy adults may have, leaving them with clear minds and well-built bodies. The school includes two volleyball courts, a football yard, and a basketball court. The school building includes gymnastics for athletes to keep training while they are staying in the school. The remaining space is covered with green grasses that have a positive effect on the students', keeping them up for studying and brainstorming.

School Residence

The student residence is divided into two main buildings, one for each gender, where each building contains four floors, twelve rooms on each one. The fifth floor includes special rooms for school workers, the dining hall, and the mosque. Each room can hold up to four students, contains four beds, 4 wardrobes, and a bathroom for each individual room. All floors have wide studying rooms equipped with a stable Wi-Fi connection, a suitable environment for students to share their thoughts and exchange ideas.

School Laps

Each Lab has its own unique look and architecture that inspires the students with thoughts more productive thoughts about the subject they are learning about. All the practical experiments are held in these labs with a variety of tools and models that illustrates these experiments. Each lab has a guiding teacher for tutoring students to hold experiments safely following the whole rules of safety and scientific methods. The school building includes 6 Labs which are:

Physics Lab

Wheter Fluids or Thermodynamics, what you can measure is not like what you can understand.

Mechanics Lab

Velocity or speed, it doesn't matter as long as you move.

Chemistry Lab

Bases and Acids, Flasks and Beaks. It is chemistry around us.

Fab Lab

This is where you can make anything!

Biology Lab

Skeletons, bacteria and microscopes. Biology is everywhere.

Geology Lab

Here where you can find out about Earth.

Campus Life

The school is a complete holistic experience. A diverse community, a research-based and curriculum-based hybrid education system, and extracurricular activities. Including students from different cultures, the campus can be considered as a fertile environment for assembling and generate new scientifically based thoughts. Everyone is longing for new experiences. The main social scene could be described as a large family.
